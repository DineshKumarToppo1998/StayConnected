# StayConnected v0.1 (BETA)


Ever had that moment where you suddenly realize you haven’t called someone important in a while… and then comes the “ab yaad aaya?” 😅

StayConnected is a simple Android app that helps you stay in touch with the people who matter—without overcomplicating things.

It quietly reminds you to call friends, family, or anyone you care about, keeps track of your call history, and even shows streaks so you don’t lose touch over time.

What it does:
- Reminds you to call people on a schedule you choose
- Tracks when you last spoke and how often you stay in touch
- Shows simple stats and streaks to keep you consistent
- Lets you snooze or act on reminders quickly
- Works entirely offline — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking

Everything is stored locally on your device. No login, no backend, no unnecessary data collection.

Why this exists

Life gets busy. People slip through the cracks—not because we don’t care, but because we forget.

This app is just a small nudge to help fix that.

Tech (for devs)
- Kotlin + Jetpack Compose (Material 3)
- MVVM + Repository pattern
- Room (SQLite) for local storage
- WorkManager for reminders
- Hilt for dependency injection

Philosophy
- Keep it local
- Keep it simple
- Keep relationships alive
